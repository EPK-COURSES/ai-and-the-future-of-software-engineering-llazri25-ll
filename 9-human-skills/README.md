## Topic 9 — Human Skills That Remain Important in the AI Era (topic-9-human-skills-that-remain-important-in-the-ai-era)

Students will investigate human-centered skills that continue to be valuable despite advances in AI.

Possible areas:

* communication,  
* teamwork,  
* leadership,  
* creativity,  
* ethical reasoning,  
* decision making,  
* problem solving,  
* client interaction,  
* systems thinking.

Main question:

* Which software engineering skills are difficult for AI to replace, and why?
