## Topic 5 — AI Statistics and Industry Reports (topic-5-ai-statistics-and-industry-reports)

Students will investigate statistics, surveys, and industry reports related to AI in software engineering.

Possible sources:

* GitHub  
* Stack Overflow  
* McKinsey & Company  
* Deloitte

Possible areas:

* AI adoption rates,  
* productivity improvements,  
* developer opinions,  
* company investments,  
* future predictions.

Students should interpret and discuss the meaning of the statistics, not only collect numbers.
