## Topic 7 — Future of Software Engineering Jobs (topic-7-future-of-software-engineering-jobs)

Students will investigate how AI may change software engineering careers and job expectations.

Possible areas:

* changing junior developer roles,  
* future expectations from senior engineers,  
* emerging AI-related engineering roles,  
* automation of repetitive tasks,  
* human skills which are difficult to automate.

Students should focus on role transformation rather than simplistic “AI replaces programmers” discussions.
