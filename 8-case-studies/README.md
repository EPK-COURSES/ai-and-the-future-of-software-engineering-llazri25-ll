## Topic 8 — Real Industry Case Studies (topic-8-real-industry-case-studies)

Students will investigate real companies integrating AI into software engineering workflows.

Possible examples:

* Microsoft  
* Google  
* Shopify  
* Duolingo

Students should analyze:

* how companies use AI,  
* workflow changes,  
* advantages,  
* limitations,  
* and challenges experienced by development teams.
